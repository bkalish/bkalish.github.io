The Spinning Reel

Coming December 2014!

A collection of 40 original tunes by Benjamin Kalish, The Spinning Reel includes reels, jigs, marches, waltzes, and polkas as well as a few tunes that can not be so easily labeled. The tunes are notated for treble instruments and chord suggestions are included for rhythm players.

About the composer

Benjamin Kalish grew up in Great Barrington, MA, and was surrounded by music from an early age. He enjoys contra dancing, English Country Dance, fiddle tunes, and good chocolate. His instrument of choice is the mandolin but he enjoys playing any instrument he can get his hands on, from fiddle to bassoon.
